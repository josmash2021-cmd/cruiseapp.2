Navatar Sprite Generator

Generates 8-angle PNG sprites for each car model | Ready for Flutter (iOS + Android)

How this works: Renders each 3D car model from 8 angles (0, 45, 90... 315 degrees) as transparent PNG sprites.
These drop into assets/images/navatars/ in your Flutter project, replacing the current 2D car sprites.
Output: 6 cars x 8 angles = 48 PNG sprites + 6 GLB files for 3D viewer
